SCC Resource Explorer

SCC Resource Explorer is an online interactive bibliographic database that offers the possibility of approaching the history of the Congregation of the Council through archival material, works of juridical doctrine and specialist scientific literature published between the 16th century and today.

Database

The catalogue contains the bibliography, related to a wide spectrum of the Congregation of the Council activities. It is ongoing project.

Methodology

Our catalogue is based on the hierachical structure of the keywords and contains a variety of categories, including archival references.

About us

SCC Resource Explorer is a project of the Max Planck Research Group “Governance of the Universal Church after the Council of Trent”.

SCC Resource Explorer is an interactive bibliographic database dedicated to the Congregation of the Council and the Council of Trent. The database is actually composed by more than 3.300 entries, related to historical and juridical aspects of the Congregation and Roman Curia. The database is constantly enriched with new entries in a collaborative way with the international scientific community. The web app offers advanced searching options, multiple graphic visualization of the search results and multiformat downloading opportunities of the data set.

Content of the Database
Texts and more

The core of this project is the bibliographical dataset, which consists of more than 3.300 names, including primary sources, books, articles, catalogues. This collection consists of the materials, selected by the research group members within their personal projects. The group page provides the further information about these topics.

Therefore, these titles correspond to a large variety of subject: from the issues of the matrimonial law to the geographical concepts of the Congregation.

How we collect the data
Approaches and methods

In accordance with the hierarchical character of the Congregation of the Council’s subjects, our bibliography has the hierarchical structure with multi-level keywords.

read more close

The keyword tree, which we used in the Smart Search and Graphs, consists of the several levels, from very general ones like ‘Roman Curia’, to specific words in order to provide the precise filters.

The keywords sections are divided by colours for a comfortable visual navigation. In addition, the search options include the search by character and numeric custom inputs, such as author, title, publsher and year.
